Title: Eight Elvises
Artist: Andy Warhol
Year: 1963




Buy this Piece!

Description: Andy Warhol is generally regarded as the best pop artist of all time. He certainly is the most famous and successful artist of this genre. In 1963 Mr. Warhol created Eight Elvises. As one might perceive from the title, this painting features the pop singer and idol of the late 1950's up until his untimely passing in 1977. This silkscreen was one of many produced by Warhol, depicting cultural icons of the period. Other similar images included those of Marilyn Monroe, Elizabeth Taylor and Michael Jackson. In 2009, Eight Elvises was purchased by a private collector for a reported $100 million. This made it one of the most expensive paintings ever sold.
