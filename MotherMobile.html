Title: Whistler's Mother
Artist: James Whistler
Year: 1871
Buy this Piece!

Description: Arrangement in Grey and Black No. 1, best known under its colloquial name Whistler's Mother, is a painting in oils on canvas created by the American-born painter James McNeill Whistler in 1871. The subject of the painting is Whistler's mother, Anna McNeill Whistler. The painting is 56.81 by 63.94 inches (1,443 mm x 1,624 mm), displayed in a frame of Whistler's own design.
